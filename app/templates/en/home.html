Welcome in Verbs

This site helps you revise irregular verbs in English and Dutch.
Different modes are available :
- Translation: translate the verb into the other language.
- Infinitive: find the infinitive of the verb.
- Past participle: find the past participle of the verb.
- Imperfect tense: find the imperfect tense of the verb.
